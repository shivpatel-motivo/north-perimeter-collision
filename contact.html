Our Location

We are conveniently located in Chamblee off I-285 about a mile inside the perimeter near the intersection of Peachtree Industrial Blvd. and North Peachtree Rd.

From I-285 you will take exit 31-A southbound. You will go about a mile and turn right onto North Peachtree Rd. ( traffic light at the corner of the CVS and Tune Up Clinic) be prepared to make an immediate left down our driveway directly across from the CVS.

Heading North on Peachtree Industrial Blvd. from Atlanta you will turn left onto North Peachtree Rd. and make an immediate left down the 1st driveway to the left across from CVS.

Heading South on Peachtree Industrial Blvd. you will go inside I-285 about a mile and turn right onto North Peachtree Rd. (traffic light at CVS and Tune up Clinic) You will then take the immediate left down the first driveway across from CVS.

Please Note: We are located down a long driveway which we share with a towing company.
